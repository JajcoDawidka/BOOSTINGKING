Welcome to our **Booster** team! Thank you for being with us and supporting our community. The following regulations outline the rules for our cooperation to ensure everything runs smoothly and satisfactorily for all parties. Remember, your role is crucial for our growth!

---

1. General Principles of Cooperation

  • **Professionalism and Integrity:** As a Booster, you represent our community. We expect professionalism, honesty, and diligence in every action you take.
  • **Communication:** We're open to your questions, suggestions, and concerns. Regularly check communication channels (e.g., Discord, email, booster panel) and actively participate in discussions when necessary.
  • **Confidentiality:** Treat all information regarding orders, users, and internal processes as confidential, and do not share them with third parties.
---

2. Order Execution and Time Management

  • **Order Acceptance:** By accepting an order, you confirm that you have the necessary skills and **sufficient time** to complete it as expected. Remember, accepting an order means you **must dedicate a significant amount of time to it**. If you're unable to commit to an order, please do not accept it.
  • **Quality of Execution:** We are committed to providing the highest quality services. Every order should be completed carefully and according to the established guidelines.
  • **Order Specific Timelines:**
    • **Duo Orders (Boosting with a Client):** After accepting a duo order, you **must contact the client** to introduce yourself and jointly agree on convenient times and a schedule for playing together. Efficient communication in this aspect is crucial.
    • **Solo Orders (Boosting on a Client's Account):** After accepting a solo order, you have a **maximum of 1 hour to begin it**. Failure to start the order within this timeframe may result in consequences.
  • **Problems and Dispute Resolution:** If you encounter any problems during the order's execution, contact us immediately. We will help you find a solution. Please report any disputes with clients to us so we can mediate.
---

3. Payments and Settlements

  • **Remuneration:** Details regarding remuneration for individual orders can be found in the booster panel next to each task.
  • **Payouts:** Payouts are processed according to the agreed schedule, after orders are completed and verified. Ensure your payout details are correctly configured.
  • **Taxes:** You are responsible for settling your taxes on earned income in accordance with applicable law.
---

4. Community Rules and Penalties

  • **Respect Others:** Treat other boosters, clients, and team members with respect. We are one community.
  • **No Cheating:** Any attempts at cheating, manipulation, or dishonest practices will result in immediate termination of cooperation.
  • **Account Security:** Protect your booster panel login credentials. Do not share them with anyone.
  • **Consequences for Failure to Fulfill Tasks:** Failure to fulfill your duties, including **neglecting accepted orders**, not starting solo boosts within the specified time, or inadequate communication with clients, may result in **penalties**.
