Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2025

At BoostingKing, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and boosting services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email, username, and other data provided during registration.
  • Order Information: Game account details necessary to complete your boosting order (e.g., rank, login credentials, progress).
  • Payment Data: Collected and processed via trusted third-party services. We do not store payment card details.
  • Usage Data: Device type, IP address, browser, visited pages (for analytics and security).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and manage your boosting orders
  • To communicate updates about your order or account
  • To improve our services and website performance
  • To prevent fraud and ensure account security
  • To provide responsive customer support

We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Protection

We use advanced security protocols and internal restrictions to protect your personal data. Game account credentials are only visible to verified boosters and are deleted after the service is completed.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our site uses cookies to enhance user experience and collect anonymous analytics. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Sharing Information

We only share your data when necessary — for example, with a trusted booster assigned to your order or with a payment processor to complete your transaction.

6. Your Rights

  • You may access the data we store about you
  • You can request correction or deletion of your information
  • You can withdraw consent to data processing at any time

To request data changes or deletion, contact us at boostingking.help@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page and marked with a new effective date.
