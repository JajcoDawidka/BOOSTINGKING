Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a trial period?
Yes – 5 test orders to verify your speed and reliability.
How and when do I get paid?
Weekly payouts every Monday. Methods: PayPal or cryptocurrency USDT. Minimum withdrawal: $50.
What's the application process?
After submitting your application, we'll review your profile and may request a trial game. The entire process takes 2-3 days.
What’s your commission rate?
We take 20%–30% (lower for elite boosters). Example: $100 order → You earn $70–$80.
Do I talk to clients?
Only via our secured chat system (no personal Discord/Skype). Follow scripted responses for professionalism.
Do I need to be available full-time?
You're not required to be full-time, but we expect active boosters to take pending orders in a timely manner. The more active you are, the more you can earn.
