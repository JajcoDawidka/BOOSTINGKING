CS2 Game Modes Boosting - FAQ

What game modes can I boost in CS2?
We currently offer boosting services for Premier and Faceit. Coming soon: Premier Placement Matches, Wingman, and Competitive matchmaking.
How does boosting differ between Faceit, Premier, and other modes?
Each mode has its own ranking system and gameplay structure. Faceit uses an ELO-based system independent from CS2, while Premier uses the official CS2 rating. Wingman and Competitive are more casual, but still offer rank progression.
Which mode is best for fast rank improvement?
Premier boosting usually results in faster visible rank changes. However, Faceit is preferred by competitive players for skill development and long-term credibility.
Can I boost multiple modes at the same time?
Yes! You can order boosts for multiple modes simultaneously. We'll coordinate your order to ensure smooth and efficient delivery across all selected modes.
Can I play with the booster instead of giving account access (DuoQ)?
Yes, DuoQ is available for selected modes like Faceit and Premier. This allows you to play together with a booster on your own account while still ranking up.
Is my account safe during the boosting process?
Yes, your security is our top priority. We never use any third-party software or cheats. Your account is only accessed by verified boosters, and DuoQ is available if you prefer full control.
Will I receive updates on my boost progress?
Yes, we provide live status updates via your customer dashboard and/or Discord. You’ll always know what stage your boost is at.
Can I pause or schedule my boost for specific times?
Yes, just let us know your preferred time windows. We’re flexible and can adapt the schedule to match your availability or avoid certain hours.
