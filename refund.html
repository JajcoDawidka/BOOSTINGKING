Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 6, 2025

At BoostingKing, we strive to provide the best possible service. However, we understand that situations may arise where a refund is necessary. Please read our refund policy carefully before placing an order.

1. Eligibility for Refund

  • Full refund: If your order has not started and no booster has been assigned.
  • Partial refund: If your order is in progress but not yet completed.
  • Full refund after completion: In certain cases, we may grant a full refund even after the order is finished — this depends on the situation and is handled case-by-case.

We always strive to be fair and transparent. If you believe you deserve a refund for any reason, contact us and we’ll carefully review your case.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team within 48 hours of your purchase. You must include your order ID, email address, and reason for the request. Refunds are typically processed within 3–7 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • If your game account was banned, restricted, or locked during or after the service due to violation of the game's terms.
  • If the account information you provided was incorrect or incomplete.
  • If you actively used the account during the boost without our permission.

4. Chargebacks & Disputes

Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first will result in a permanent ban from our services. We are always willing to cooperate and resolve any issues fairly.

5. Service Satisfaction

If you’re not satisfied with the progress or experience, please reach out. Our support team is happy to assist, reassign a booster, or find a fair resolution before considering a refund.
